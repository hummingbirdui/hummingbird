###### Scrolling long content

When modals become too long for the user’s viewport or device, they scroll independent of the page itself. Try the demo below to see what we mean.

<Example code={false}>
  <div slot="preview">
    <button type="button" class="btn btn-primary" data-bs-toggle="modal" data-bs-target="#modalScrollable">Demo modal</button>

    <div class="modal fade" id="modalScrollable" tabindex="-1" aria-labelledby="modalScrollableLabel" aria-hidden="true">
      <div class="modal-dialog">
        <div class="modal-content">
          <div class="modal-header">
            <h1 class="modal-title text-base font-semibold" id="modalScrollableLabel">Modal title</h1>
            <button type="button" class="btn btn-icon ms-auto" data-bs-dismiss="modal" aria-label="Close">
              <span class="icon-[material-symbols--close-rounded] text-base"></span>
            </button>
          </div>
          <div class="modal-body min-h-screen">
            {`This is a modal window. You can add any content here—text, buttons, forms, or media.`}
          </div>
          <div class="modal-footer">
            <button type="button" class="btn btn-subtle-neutral me-2" data-bs-dismiss="modal">Close</button>
            <button type="button" class="btn btn-subtle-primary">Confirm</button>
          </div>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>

  </div>
</Example>
